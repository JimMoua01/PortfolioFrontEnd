What did you take away from your projects?

Building model kits is something I should do only when I feel like it. While I’m proud of how all the kits turned out, I won’t deny that I got burnt out. The process of cutting out parts, assembling them, panel lining, and applying stickers got monotonous. In the future, I should space out my kits and dedicate time to each of them, so that I can fully enjoy the process.

Gunpla

Gunpla is short for Gundam plastic model kit. What sets it apart from other model kits is that it's based on the long-running franchise, Mobile Suit Gundam, and it's building process is snap-build, meaning no glue is required. Gunpla kits are usually grouped into four main grades, which denote it's size, complexity, and detail.

    • High Grade
